Our Basic Terms and Conditions

Welcome to Camps Rishikesh Inn! Please review the following terms and conditions before booking or using our services. Your use of our website and services implies your agreement to adhere to these terms.

Booking and Payment

  • Check-in time is at 13:00 pm, and check-out time is at 10:00 am.
  • Early check-ins are subject to availability.
  • Late check-outs are not permitted. Guests wishing to extend their stay must book the room for the entire day.
  • 100% payment is required at check-in.

Cancellation Policy

The advance amount for bookings is non-refundable in case of cancellation.

Damage to Property

Guests will be held responsible for any damage caused to the property during their stay, and charges will apply accordingly.

Identification and Prohibited Items

  • All guests must possess valid identification cards, which should be presented upon request.
  • Firearms, illegal weapons, and drugs are strictly prohibited on the property.
  • No alcoholic beverages are allowed in open and public areas.
  • Smoking and fireworks are not permitted inside the tents and public areas.
  • Pets are not allowed on the premises.

Safety and Security

Guests are responsible for the safety of their valuable items such as purses, mobile phones, and jewelry. We do not guarantee the security of guests' belongings.

Refund Policy

No refund will be provided if a guest checks out before the designated check-out time unless there is an issue from our end.
