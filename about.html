About Us

Under the brand name “Kylo Resort,” ROCK HIMALAYAN HERBAL PRIVATE LIMITED has introduced an innovative short-stay accommodation concept, branded as “The Natural Luxe Retreat.” We offer a distinctive glamping experience that combines the rustic charm of camping with the convenience and safety of modern luxury amenities. At “The Natural Luxe Retreat,” we cater to a diverse range of guests, regardless of age, gender, or adventure preferences. Our glamping site promises a host of unique features: Premium Dome Accommodations: Enjoy an ultra-luxurious stay with our stylish domes that blend glamour and nature seamlessly. Café and Dining Area: Delight in delicious cuisine in a café-cum-dining area, where positive vibes and sumptuous meals abound. Serene Natural Surroundings: Immerse yourself in lush green surroundings that create the perfect atmosphere for a relaxing getaway. Sunrise Overlooking the Mountains: Witness breathtaking sunrise views from our vantage point overlooking the mountain valley. Community Gathering Area: Connect with fellow guests in our common area, adorned with fairy lights and soothing music. Bonfire Experience: Experience the raw beauty of a bonfire with natural seating, adding a touch of wilderness to your stay. Nature-Inspired Swimming Pool: Take a dip in our swimming pool while enjoying unobstructed views of the natural beauty surrounding you. Open-Air Cinema: Enjoy your favorite music, movies, or karaoke in our open-air cinema, a unique entertainment experience under the stars. Live Performances and Open Mic: Showcase your talent or enjoy live performances on our open stage, fostering a sense of community. Photobooths with Valley Views: Capture unforgettable moments against the backdrop of the valley, lit up with glitzy lights. “The Natural Luxe Retreat” at Kylo Resort offers an unforgettable glamping experience that combines the best of nature and luxury for a truly exceptional stay.
