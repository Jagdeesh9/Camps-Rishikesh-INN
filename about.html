Camps Rishikesh Inn

About the property

Nestled in the picturesque valley of Shivpuri, renowned as the starting point for rafting adventures and a charming town near Rishikesh, lies Camp Rishikesh Inn. This esteemed camping resort is celebrated for its exceptional offerings and idyllic setting.

The property boasts an array of amenities designed to enhance guests' experiences, including an inviting in-house restaurant where culinary delights await. Whether savoring local delicacies or international fare, guests are treated to a culinary journey that tantalizes the taste buds.

For those seeking relaxation and rejuvenation, a refreshing swimming pool beckons, providing a tranquil oasis amidst the natural beauty of the surroundings. Meanwhile, the lush green gardens offer a serene retreat, perfect for leisurely strolls or quiet moments of reflection.

Families and friends can delight in the expansive playing area, where laughter fills the air as guests engage in spirited games and activities. Here, cherished memories are forged against the backdrop of scenic vistas and warm hospitality.

At Camp Rishikesh Inn, our commitment to excellence shines through in every aspect of our offerings. With a focus on quality over quantity, we strive to provide guests with an unforgettable experience that exceeds expectations and leaves a lasting impression.

What do we offer?

Indulge in the epitome of luxury camping at Camp Rishikesh Inn, where each tent offers breathtaking views of the majestic Mount and the sacred waters of the Ganges. Surrounded by expansive lush green gardens, guests are invited to immerse themselves in nature's embrace alongside their loved ones

ide the tent, you'll find spacious accommodations spanning 130 square feet, featuring two queen-size beds adorned with plush bedding for a restful night's sleep. Stay cool and comfortable with the provided air cooler, ensuring a tranquil atmosphere even during the warmest days. A bedside table offers convenience for keeping essentials close at hand, while complimentary water bottles and a charging point ensure your comfort and connectivity throughout your stay.

Step into the sanitized attached toilet, boasting an ample 50 square feet of space, where basic toiletries are provided for your convenience and comfort.

Venture outside your luxurious abode to discover a wealth of amenities and recreational opportunities. Take a refreshing dip in the inviting swimming pool, surrounded by verdant greenery that adds to the ambiance of relaxation. Explore the sprawling green garden area, perfect for leisurely walks and picnics amidst the serene surroundings.

For those seeking active pursuits, the playing area and indoor gaming zone offer entertainment for guests of all ages. Whether engaging in friendly competition or simply enjoying quality time together, these spaces provide endless opportunities for fun and laughter.

When hunger strikes, head to the restaurant area, where delectable culinary creations await. Savor a diverse array of dishes while soaking in the picturesque views of the surrounding landscape, creating unforgettable dining experiences to cherish.

At Camp Rishikesh Inn, luxury meets nature, offering guests an unparalleled retreat where every moment is infused with comfort, beauty, and adventure.
