Bungee in Rishikesh

Bungee jumping in Rishikesh is an extreme and one of the most popular adventure activities. In Rishikesh, you will get India’s highest bungee jump, which was 83 metres until 2023. After 2023, India’s highest bungee is 107 metres, which is also located in Rishikesh. Rishikesh has a total of four bunges now. Jumpin Heights (83 Mitrs), Thrill Factory (55 Mitrs), Splash Bungee (109 Mitrs), and Himalayan Bungee (107 Mitrs). All the bungee jumping spots are tested and safe, so you can choose which suits you best.

Bungee Jumping spot in Rishikesh

Thrill Factory 55 Mitrs

This is the smallest bungee tower in Rishikesh with only 55 mitrs of height. Thrill Fectory is located in Shivpuri which is 15KM far from Rishikesh. Thrill fectro provide Bungee Jumping, Tendom Bungee, Revers Bungee, Gaint Swint, Zipline, Bowling and 15+ more adventure activites in 1 place. The price for this 55 Mitrs of Bungee is 3000 per person and the price of video recording for this bungee is 900 per person

Jumpin Heights 83 Mitrs

Jumpin Heights is the oldest bungee jumping spot in Rishikesh. The height of this bungee is 83 metres, and this is the only bungee that is made in a natural structure with a valley view. Other bungees are made on towers. but this one is made on a mountain with a platform. Jumping heights also provide gaint swing and flaying fox (Asia’s Longest Flying Fox). The price of this is 83 Mitrs on Bungee at 3700 per person, with an additional 100 rupees as entry fees. For video recording, they charge 900 per person.

Splash Bungee 109 Mitrs

The Splash Bungee is located in Shivpuri; this is the only water-based bungee available in India/Rishikesh. The tower height of this bungee is 109 metres and gives a thrilling experience while jumping from a platform. You can experience water splashing in this bungee, which gives you the unique experience of bungee and water activity together. The price of this bungee is 4000 per person, and the photo or video recording is also included in the price.

Himalayan Bungy 107 Mitrs

The Himalayan Bungee is India's highest bungee, with 107 metres of height between ground and jumping platform and 117 metres of tower height. This extreme bungee gives the best thrilling experience due to its height. The Himalayan bungee is located in Shivpuri, which is 15 km from Rishikesh. The price for this bungee is 4600 per person, and photos and video recording charges are 1500 per person.
