Camping in Rishikesh

Rishikeh is known for its natural beauty and comes under the foothills of the Himalayas. The majestic mountains and Ganga River make it the perfect camping location. You can choose to camp by the river and hear the water flowing, or you can camp in the jungle with all the awesome nature around you. There are multiple options available for different types of experience. The best part of camping in Rishikesh is the bonfire night with light music. You also get delicious meals, Instagrameble photos, and video spots. You can have a good time with your friends, family, or loved ones.

Types of Camping In rishikesh

Dormitory

Dormitory is the perfect option for a solo traveller, in which you just need to pay to go to bed. which means if you are solo, you don't need to pay for the whole camp or room; you just need to pay for the bed. Rishikesh Doms charges around 500–900 per bed; the other option is Dhramshala, which is going to cost you 300–600 per bed

Beach Camping

Beach camps are specifically located along sandy riverbanks or shores, creating a setting similar to a beach experience. Beach camps build a connection between you and nature through sound, sand, and the environment. These types of camps are specially designed for nature lovers who just want to have a raw experience. Beach camps have been banned in Rishikesh since 2015, but right now you will get beach camps at Shivpuri. but only 4–5 camp sites are available with limited resources and strict guidelines (no music, no swimming pool, common washroom, coom light, etc.). The average price of a beach camp is 999 per person. for triple or quart sharing and 1200 per person for double sharing.

River side Camping

TThese camps are typically situated along the banks of a river, like the Ganges, Yul, and other local small rivers in Rishikesh. You can spend a good time at the riverside. Most of the riverside camps are located in Gattu Ghat, Ratta Pani, and Garud Chatti, where you can go for a waterfall trek. Some of the riverside camps are also located in Shivpuri. The average price of Riverside Camps is 1,000 per person for triple or quad sharing and 1200 per person for double sharing

Luxury Camping

Luxury camps are the ones that provide all the facilities for camping. Luxury camps, also known as glamping, are where you get all the facilities like a swimming pool, an attached washroom in every camp, high-quality tents, and other indore activities. Luxury camps are rarely available near the bank of the river; most of the luxury camps are located in Shivpuri, and some of them are on Neelkanth Road. The price of a luxury camp in Rishikesh is 1200 per person for triple or quad sharing and 1400 per person for double sharing.

AC Cottages

The AC cottage is a wall of fibres and looks like a room. These are the upgraded versions of a luxury camp with a water heater and AC in it. the capacity of cottages is 4-6 person and the Price on AC cottages in Rishikesh is 1800 Per person on Triple/quad sharing and 2200PP on Double sharing

Frequently Asked Questions

CAN YOU PROVIDE US PICKUP AND DROP ?

No pickup and drop not available for camping; you have to come by yourself to our campsite.

HOW TO REACH SHIVPURI FOR CAMPING?

From Rishikesh, you can come to Shivpuri campsite via private vehicles,public transport (buses), cabs, and rental vehicles.

PARKING AVAILABLE NEAR CAMPS?

Yes, most of the campsites have paid parking nearby, which will cost you 50–100 per day.

ARE PETS ALLOW IN CAMPS ?

Pets are not allowed in camping, but you can ask the camp manager for more details.

WHAT IS THE TIMING OF MUSIC AND BONFIRE?

As per the rules Music last time is 10PM which is fixed. and same for the bonfire.

CHECKIN AND CHECKOUT TIMEING OF CAMPING?

Generally our Check In timing is 10-11AM and last Check in time is 8PM and our checkout timings are 9AM-11AM.
