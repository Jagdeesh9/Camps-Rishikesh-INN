Kayaking course in Rishikesh

Beginner to advanced 30 days Kayaking course

This 30-day kayaking course is designed for both those who have already done a beginner kayaking course and those who are just starting to learn kayaking from scratch. You will learn swimming, rescue techniques, kayaking drills, rolling, teamwork in kayaking, and much more. After completing this course, you will be able to kayak at the Grade III+ level of rapid and have kayaking fun in white water. You will get all the equipment, like a kayak jacket, helmet, paddel, rescue bag, and much more. every day for 2-4 hours of river time with your instructor. The price for this course is 1500 rupees per day. Note: Stay and food are not included in this price; for that, you have to pay an extra 1,000 INR per day.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much time its take to learn Kayaking in Rishikesh

It totally depends on you and the time you spend on the river and some other places, but the average time is 4-5 days, and you will learn balancing, roles, and other basic techniques of kayaking.

We anyone do Kayaking in Rishikehs?

In the Rishikesh River, there are lots of rapids and white water. That’s why you need to have some experience doing direct kayaking in Rishikesh, or you can learn it from scretch.

What is the Price of Kayaking in Rishikesh?

The price for kayaking in Rishikesh is totally variable for each kayak school, but it starts from 999 per day to 2000 per day.

What is Ganga Kayak Festival?

Kanga Kayak Festival is a kayaking competition on Rishikesh conducted every year in February, in which people compete to get the title of best kayaker and also win prizes.
