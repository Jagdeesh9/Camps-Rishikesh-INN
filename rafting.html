River Rafting in Rishikesh

Rishikesh is known as the adventure capital of India, where rafting plays a major role. Rafting is one of the most popular adventure activities in Rishikesh. River rafting in Rishikesh is an exhilarating experience that combines the thrill of adventure with the natural beauty of the Himalayan region. Rafting in Rishikesh is known as the safest river rafting in the world, with thrilling grade III rapids. There are many routes for river rafting in Rishikesh, like 12 km, 16 km, 26 km, and 36 km. 16Km is the most famous section because of the Grade III rapids like the golf course, roller coaster, and clubhouse. You can choose any route or section that suits you better.
