Frequently Asked Questions

Is ziplining safe?

Yes, ziplining is generally safe when conducted by professional operators with well-maintained equipment. Always follow the safety guidelines provided by the instructors, and ensure that you are wearing the necessary safety gear.

What is the duration of a ziplining session?

Generally, a ziplining session may last anywhere from 30-45 minutes, including safety briefings and multiple zipline rides.

What should I wear for ziplining?

Wear comfortable clothing and closed-toe shoes. Avoid loose jewelry or accessories. Some operators may provide specific gear, so it’s recommended to check with them in advance.

Can I bring a camera or phone while ziplining?

It’s generally not recommended to bring valuables like cameras or phones during the ziplining experience. Operators usually provide a secure place to store your belongings before the activity.
